BTEC Higher National Diploma

The BTEC Higher National Diploma (=BTS SIO: Computer Services for Organizations in France) is a program of baccalaureate+2 level, which trains computer scientists as well gifted for the development as for the administration and maintenance of a computer network. The BTEC Higher National Diploma is divided into two distinct options: Option SISR and Option SLAM. The SISR Option (System and Network Infrastructure Solution) aims to train versatile network technicians. Graduates of this program have all the necessary skills for the management and maintenance of a company's network. The SLAM Option (Software Solutions and Business Applications) is a course dedicated to web and software development enthusiasts. During these two years of study, students learn to write specifications, develop applications and ensure their integration into the company's computerized systems.
